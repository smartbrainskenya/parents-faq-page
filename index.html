Start Here: Your Child's Student Username Read First
You need this before you can register — get it right the first time
🚨
Do not register until you have confirmed your child's username. The username is required during registration and cannot be changed easily after the fact. An incorrect username means your registration will be stuck and will need admin correction.

What is a Student Username?

Every child enrolled with Smart Brains Kenya has a unique username. It is made up of a school code (a short 2-letter abbreviation for their school) and your child's name, joined by a dash. It looks like this:

How the username is structured
School Code
—
Child's Name
=
os-justineharmon
Real examples
Office School os-justineharmon
Demo School ds-marywanjiku
Jawabu School js-coderisaac
How to find and confirm the username
1

Ask your child for their username

Your child's ICT or Coding teacher gave them a username when they enrolled. Ask your child what it is. If they've forgotten, they should check with their teacher at school.

2

Have your child test it on their student login page

Ask your child to visit smartbrainskenya.com, click Student Section, and try logging in with their username. If they can log in successfully, the username is correct and you are ready to register.

3

Write down the username exactly as it appears

Note it down carefully — all lowercase letters, no spaces, with a dash in the correct place. For example: os-justineharmon

⚠️
Do not guess the username. Only proceed to registration once you have confirmed it by logging in successfully.
💡 Once you have the confirmed username, you are ready to register.
Keep it somewhere you can copy from — you will need to type it into the registration form in the next step.

How to Register
Create your parent account — you need your child's username from Step 1
✅
You should already have your child's confirmed username from Section 1. If not, go back and complete that step first before registering.
1

Go to the Smart Brains Kenya website

Open your browser and visit smartbrainskenya.com. At the top right, tap the "Parent Section" button (blue button in the navigation bar).

2

Go to Sign Up

You will land on the login page. Since you don't have an account yet, tap "Don't have an account? Sign up today!" at the bottom of the screen.

3

Fill in your details

Complete the registration form with the following:

• First & Last Name — your name as the parent/guardian
• Email Address
• Your Role — choose Mom, Dad, or Guardian
• WhatsApp Number — enter without the leading zero. E.g. if your number is 0712345678, enter 712345678. This will also be your login username.
• Password — choose a password and confirm it
• Child's Student Username — paste in the username you confirmed in Section 1 above (e.g. os-justineharmon)
4

Agree to Terms & tap "Create Account"

Check the box agreeing to receive WhatsApp updates and the Terms & Conditions, then tap "Create Account" to submit your registration.

ℹ️
After registering, your account will be pending approval by your child's school. You will receive an SMS on your WhatsApp number once you have been approved. See Section 3 for what to expect next.
3
Getting Approved
What happens after you register
✓

Registration submitted

Your account is created and is now pending approval.

2

Follow up with your school

Contact your child's ICT teacher or Coding patron at school and let them know you have registered. They will approve your account from the school side.

3

You will receive an SMS confirmation

Once approved, you will receive an SMS on your registered WhatsApp number that reads:

"Hi [Name], your Smart Brains Kenya parent account has been approved! Login at application.smartbrainskenya.com..."
4

Your child can now access Home Projects

Once approved, your child can log in to their student dashboard, go to Home Projects, and request an access code. That code will be sent directly to your WhatsApp number.

⚠️
Did not receive the approval SMS? This usually means your phone number has promotional messages blocked. See Section 4 below to fix this.
4
Not Receiving SMS?
How to fix blocked promotional messages

If you registered but did not receive your approval SMS or your child's access code, your phone number may be on a promotional messages blacklist. This happens when a number has opted out of promotional SMS — usually by mistake or automatically by the carrier.

🚫
This affects Safaricom, Airtel, and Telkom numbers. The Smart Brains system cannot deliver any SMS to numbers that have blocked promotional messages.
How to re-enable promotional SMS:
Dial this code on your phone
*456*9*5*5#
Works on Safaricom numbers · Select "Allow All" when prompted
1

Open your phone dialler

Using the phone number you registered with, open your regular phone dialler (the one you use to make calls).

2

Dial *456*9*5*5# and press Call

A menu will appear on screen. Follow the prompts to allow all promotional messages.

3

Wait about 1 hour

After re-enabling, please wait approximately 1 hour for the changes to take effect across the network.

4

Ask your child to request the access code again

After the 1-hour wait, have your child log in to their student dashboard, go to Home Projects, and request a new access code. It should now arrive on your number.

✅
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact your school's ICT/Coding patron or reach out to Smart Brains Kenya support directly.

Home Projects & Submission New
How to view and submit your child's project for review
📌
Once your account is approved and your child has completed their home project, you as the parent need to submit it for tutor review through your dashboard.
Step-by-step: Submitting your child's project
1

Log in to your Parent Dashboard

Visit application.smartbrainskenya.com, enter your phone number (without leading zero) and your password, then tap Submit.

2

Tap "Projects" from the bottom navigation

At the bottom of your screen you will see Home, Reports, Projects, and Gallery. Tap Projects.

3

Select the correct project

If you have more than one child linked to your account, you will see multiple projects listed. Tap on the project for the child whose work you want to submit.

🔵 In Progress
🟡 Pending Review

A project showing "In Progress" is ready to be submitted. "Pending Review" means it has already been submitted and is awaiting the tutor.

4

Leave feedback (optional)

You can tap on Feedback to view messages between you and the tutor, or to send a message about your child's project.

5

Tap "Submit for Tutor Review"

Tap the purple "Submit for Tutor Review" button. A confirmation screen will appear — you can optionally add a note to the tutor. Tap "Submit for Review" to confirm.

⚠️
Note: Once submitted, your child will not be able to make changes to the project until the tutor responds.
Adding a Second Child to Your Account
1

Log in and go to your Home screen

Once logged in, you'll see an "Add Child" button at the top right of your home screen.

2

Enter your second child's username

A pop-up will appear asking for the Child Username and your relationship to them. Enter the username (e.g. ds-marywanjiku) and tap "Add Child".

ℹ️
You can only register one child during the initial sign-up. Additional children can be added after logging in using the Add Child button on the home screen.
